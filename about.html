About Us

Welcome to Paintique – a platform for creative souls who bring color and emotions to life through sketches and paintings.

It started with a passion for art and creativity. We believe every wall deserves a story, and every heart deserves custom-made memories. Whether you're looking for original artwork, printed frames, or personalized sketches, we bring your vision to reality.

Our mission is to support young artists, provide a place for custom orders, and inspire creativity in every corner of your home.

Meet the Founder

A self-taught artist with a dream of turning creativity into a community. Sketching, painting, and helping others decorate with meaningful art is our purpose.
